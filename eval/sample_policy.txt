QABot Company Remote Work Policy

Effective date: January 2024

Overview
All employees may work remotely up to three days per week. Remote work must be approved by the direct manager.

Core hours
Employees must be available between 10:00 and 16:00 Central European Time on remote days.

Equipment
The company provides a laptop and an external monitor for eligible remote employees. Employees are responsible for a stable internet connection.

Security
All remote access must use the company VPN. Confidential documents must not be stored on personal devices.

Support
For IT issues during remote work, contact helpdesk@qabot.example or extension 4500.

Review
This policy is reviewed annually by the People Operations team.
